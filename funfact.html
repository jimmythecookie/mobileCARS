FUN FACTS THAT YOU DIDNT KNOW ABOUT THE PIXAR MOVIE: CARS

  1. Cars is the last Pixar movie to be released on VHS, and the first Pixar movie to be released on Blu-ray.



  2. Occasionally, Lightning McQueen's tongue sticks out when he is thinking hard about something. This is something that director John Lasseter does that the animators included into the film.



  3. Lightning McQueen was originally going to be number 57 (the year director John Lasseter was born). It was changed to 95 to represent the year Toy Story was released (1995).



  4. The human inspiration for Lightning McQueen was Michael Jordan.



  5. Mater's car design is a 1955 Chevrolet Stepside tow truck.



  6. Tow Mater's license plate is A-113, which represents the number of the animation classroom at Cal Arts.



  7. The race car's tires say Lightyear, which refers to the company Goodyear and to Buzz Lightyear from Toy Story.
